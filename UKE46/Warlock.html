Warlock

Unconventional warfare

You are expected to be the mind of your team. You have high utility and you can deal damage as well. A Warlock needs to place its buffs at the right place at the right time, to either support your team or self from harm, or to make your skilled allies even better. Ikora Rey is Warlock's mentor.

Dawnblade is perhaps the most beloved super in the game. It is not particulary better than the others, but it is something special. You equp a set of wings and a sword which is burning. Whenever you slash with the sword, a rift of flames is sent from the sword, destroying everything in its way. This Sword is not for children.

You also wish you could imbue yourself with the lightning powers of Sheev Palpatine? Now you can. Well, almost. When you are the Stormcaller subclass, you can activate stormtrance to send out large waves of arc energy. This can be sent wherever you like, countinually for over 5 seconds. UNLIMITED POWER!

Voidwalker is for the Warlocks that instantly want to shut down one guy. Voidwalker gives you a super which do not put you in a special state of power. Activate Nova Bomb to unleash a large projectile of pure Void energy. This tracks down the nearest target and creates a massive explosion. Also deals high damage to enemies in the are of the explosion.
