Classes

In Destiny 2 you cannot play unless you make a Guardian. This guardian is your own. You choose how the guardian may look, you may even chose to not be a human at all. This may sound cool, but the most essential decition is yet to be made. What class will your guardian be?

You may ask why this is so important, and the simple answer is that the casses decide how your guardian will fight, its spells, its armours and its physical capabilities. Titan, Warlock and Hunter. The 3 Destiny 2 guardian classes. All classes has one unique class ability, same in all their subclasses.

The Titan is the guardian for the tanks. First in, last out. This is the perfect class for those who are good leaders. In all games you need a leader, and it is the tanks role. The Titan can certainly take a beating and deal out a good amount of damage as well. With his shield barrier as class ability, you can easily secure areas and make cover without being in danger.

Sometimes you don't feel like you can decide what class you want to be. The Warlock is a support class, that also can play like an assault at times. The Warlock boosts its allies and uses powerfull magic to outplay its enemies. With a class ability that lets you chose between two rifts. Healing rift and Empowering rift. One healing allies and the other making allies deal more damage.

If you don't feel like being a teamplayer, you could go the Hunter. The Hunter is often used with teams, but it can shine just as much on its own. The Hunter is elusive and quick, but cannot take any hits. The Hunter uses its agile capabilities to confuse enemies and eliminate them. With the Hunters class ability, Dodge, you disapear in a cloud of smoke and instantly appears somewhere near of your decition.
