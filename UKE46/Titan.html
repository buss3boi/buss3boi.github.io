Titan

first in, last out

As the titan you are supposed to be the bravest of them all. You are willing to risk yourself and be the first to enter the battlefield, to set defences for your team. You don't need any strategy when you have immense strength and strong blows. This is the menotor of the Titans, Zavala.

Ever felt like being america and just smashing people with a shield? You got it with the sentinel subclass. With the super ability Sentinel Shield, you embed yourself in Void energy, and equip a shield. This shield will hit enemies with massive power and will disintegrate them at once. You can block with the shield, and you can even throw it!

What time is it? Hammer time! The Titan has one subclass which offers a more ranged playstyle. His two other subclasses craves that you dive your enemy, but not this one. As the Sunbreaker you can activate your super Hammer of Sol. This makes you an unstoppable catapult, capable of throwing heavy hammers, imbued with flames at high speed.

Striker is unique to almost all the other supers in the game. You do not equip any weapon or make a spell to unleash at your enemies. The striker simply makes the player itself a weapon. Cover yourself with Arc energy and smash down your opponents with your bare fists. This essentially makes you superman as you already can fly some distances.
