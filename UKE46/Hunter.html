Hunter

Bring the pain

The Hunter dives the enemy and deals high damage while being a confusion. This helps you eliminate them, or let your team put them down. Your dodge ability can either recharge your melee ability or reload your weapon. Cayde 6 is a enjoyable exo character, and the mentor of the Hunters.

Arcstrider is a close combat alternative for the Hunter. It equips the Hunter with the Arc Staff. This is a staff empowered by Arc energy. This staff does not hit as hard as the Sentinels Sentinel Shield, but you can attack much faster with this weapon. To many, it resembles a bo-staff. A quick and effective weapon for melee combat, but as well as in real life, this weapon craves skill.

The Nightstalker gives you the oportunity to become the archer superhero you always see on TV! The Hunters void subclass lets you equip a bow out of void energy. This super is called the Shadowshot. This lets you shoot arrows at your enemies. It does not deal any damage, but enemies hit are put in stasis for 5 seconds. Targets are not able to look where they wish, and they cannot move or use any abilities either.

This is perhaps the Hunters only real pure bad ass subclass. The Gunslinger lets you equip the Golden Gun. This is a revolver, ully loaded with solar imbued bullets. A standard six shooter, but with bullets capable of tearing even the strongest opponents down. Even guardians can barely take more than one of these solar bullets.
